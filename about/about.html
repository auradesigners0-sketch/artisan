Our Story

2010

The Beginning

Sudama's vision for soulful spaces takes root with a passion for elegant interiors.

2015

First Collection

Launched to critical acclaim, establishing our signature aesthetic.

2020

Global Expansion

Reached international markets, bringing artisan decor to homes worldwide.

Artisan Decor was founded by Sudama, a creative entrepreneur with a passion for elegant interiors and meaningful design. With a background in visual branding and a deep appreciation for craftsmanship, Sudama built the brand to celebrate the intersection of beauty and authenticity.

What began as a personal pursuit of soulful spaces has grown into a collaborative studio—where designers, makers, and storytellers come together to create objects that feel alive.
