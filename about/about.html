Vision

To create interiors that feel like poetry—spaces where every object carries intention, beauty, and soul. We envision a world where homes are sanctuaries, filled with pieces that speak softly but leave a lasting impression. Our designs are not just furnishings—they're invitations to live more thoughtfully.

Mission

To craft timeless, artisan-made decor that elevates everyday living. We partner with skilled makers, honor natural materials, and design with emotional resonance—so that every piece tells a story and every space feels personal.

What We Believe

Design is emotional

A well-placed object can shift the mood of a room, evoke memory, and inspire calm.

Craft matters

We work with artisans who shape each piece with care, using heritage techniques and honest materials.

Simplicity is powerful

We favor clean lines, natural textures, and quiet details that speak volumes.

Spaces should reflect the soul

Your home is your canvas. We offer pieces that help you express your story with grace and intention.

Our Story

Artisan Decor was founded by Sudama, a creative entrepreneur with a passion for elegant interiors and meaningful design. With a background in visual branding and a deep appreciation for craftsmanship, Sudama built the brand to celebrate the intersection of beauty and authenticity.

What began as a personal pursuit of soulful spaces has grown into a collaborative studio—where designers, makers, and storytellers come together to create objects that feel alive.

Our Core Values

Intentionality
Every design choice is made with purpose—from materials to form to placement.
Sustainability
We source responsibly, produce in small batches, and favor longevity over excess.
Craftsmanship
We honor the hands that make—celebrating skill, tradition, and artistry.
Emotional Design
Our pieces are designed to resonate, not just decorate.
Timelessness
We don't chase trends. We create heirlooms.
